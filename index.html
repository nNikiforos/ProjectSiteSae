Arkadi Monastery

The Arkadi Monastery is an Eastern Orthodox monastery, situated on a fertile plateau 23 km to the southeast of Rethymnon.

Preveli Beach

Preveli Beach, Finikodasos is a beach located in the municipality of Agios Vasilios in the Rethymno

Triopetra Beach

Triopetrabeach owes its name to the three imposing rocks found in its waters. It is located 52 km south of Rethymno town and 13 km north of Akoumia village.

Old town Rethymno

The old town in Rethymno it is located in the heart of today's Rethymno and combines the oriental appearance of the Ottoman Empire with the Western architectural style of the Renaissance.

The Kourtaliotiko Gorge

Kourtaliotiko, is on the southern side of the western part of the island of Rethymno. It is situated where the Kourtaliotiko River flows southwards between the mountains of Kouroupa and Xiron.
